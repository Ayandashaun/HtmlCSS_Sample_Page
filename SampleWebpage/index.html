About US
New Horizons is a nongovernmental organization (NGO) that focuses on community development programs. We believe that everyone deserves to have access to the resources they need to live a healthy, happy, and productive life. That's why we work to empower communities to improve their own lives through education, healthcare, economic development, and other essential services.
